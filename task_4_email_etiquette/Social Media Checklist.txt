 Social Media Checklist
Do'sDon'tsDo audit your privacy settings regularly to ensure only intended audiences see personal posts.Don't post content depicting illegal activities, excessive partying, or unprofessional behavior.Do use platforms like LinkedIn to showcase academic projects, certifications, and volunteer work.Don't engage in "cyber-arguments" or use inflammatory language that could be traced back to you.Do Google yourself periodically to see what information is publicly available to strangers.Don't share sensitive personal data like your student ID number, home address, or current GPS location.Do verify the authenticity of news or "viral" claims before sharing them to avoid spreading misinformation.Don't badmouth current or former professors, employers, or classmates online.Do use a professional-looking headshot and a clean "About Me" bio on your public-facing profiles.Don't tag others in compromising photos without their explicit consent.

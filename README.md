## 📌 Project Overview

This repository is my **Digital Literacy Portfolio**, submitted as part of the CSE0001 course at VIT Bhopal University. The project is built around a real-world scenario — acting as a **Student Digital Ambassador** — and covers five modules that together build a strong foundation in digital skills, professional online presence, platform awareness, communication etiquette, and cybersecurity.

All tasks are documented here in the repository, and a full written report is available in the `report/` folder.

## ✅ Module Summaries

### 📊 Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
> **Tool Used:** Canva
> **Module 1 | 20 Marks**

Created a one-page visual infographic covering the meaning of digital literacy, safe internet practices, professional online presence, and useful digital tools for students. Designed to be shared with batchmates as part of the Digital Ambassador role.

📂 [`task-1-presentation/`](./task-1-presentation/)

### 🌐 Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
> **Platforms:** GitHub · LinkedIn · Kaggle
> **Module 2 | 20 Marks**

Set up a foundational professional presence across three platforms. Created a GitHub profile README with name, branch, year, and learning goals. Updated the LinkedIn Education section and created a Kaggle profile to begin the journey in data science and ML competitions.

📂 [`task-2-portfolio/`](./task-2-portfolio/)

### 💻 Task 3 – Coding & Collaboration Platforms
> **Platforms:** HackerRank · Google Forms

Completed a beginner coding challenge on HackerRank to get familiar with competitive programming environments. Also built a 5-question **Digital Literacy Awareness Quiz** using Google Forms — a practical tool that can be used to quiz batchmates in my Digital Ambassador capacity.

### ✉️ Task 4 – Professional Email & Etiquette Guide
> **Module 4 | 20 Marks**

Drafted two professional emails — one to a professor requesting a deadline extension, and one to an internship coordinator expressing interest in a summer internship. Also created a social media Do's and Don'ts checklist tailored for college students.

### 🔐 Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness Case Study
> **Topic:** UPI / Online Payment Fraud
> **Module 5 | 20 Marks**

Researched and wrote a detailed case study on UPI payment fraud, a cybercrime highly relevant to college students in India. Also prepared a practical **Stay Safe Online** checklist with 8+ actionable tips, including UPI-specific safety advice and the National Cyber Crime helpline details.
